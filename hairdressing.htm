Course Overview

This comprehensive Hairdressing course equips learners with professional skills in hair cutting, styling, coloring, and treatment. Designed for individuals passionate about the beauty industry, the program provides hands-on training in modern salon techniques, customer service, hygiene, and product knowledge. Participants gain experience in client consultation, scalp care, braiding, and equipment handling, enabling them to work in salons or launch their own hairdressing businesses.
