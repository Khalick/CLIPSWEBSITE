Course Overview

This comprehensive course equips learners with essential skills in environmental hygiene, cleaning protocols, infection prevention, and effective waste management specifically tailored for healthcare settings. Designed for those pursuing careers or entrepreneurial ventures in hospitals, clinics, NGOs, and sanitation services, the program empowers graduates to apply industry-standard cleaning and disinfection processes, implement infection prevention and control (IPC) measures, and safely manage healthcare waste. Participants will also gain expertise in proper PPE usage, occupational safety compliance, and professional communication within health facilities. Additionally, the course provides valuable insights into entrepreneurship, enabling learners to explore self-employment opportunities in the growing sanitation and hygiene sector.
