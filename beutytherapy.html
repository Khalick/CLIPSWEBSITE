Course Overview

The Beauty Therapy course offers hands-on training in facial treatments, body massage, nail technology, skincare, waxing, and makeup. Designed for aspiring beauty professionals, the program equips learners with essential knowledge in anatomy, salon ethics, product usage, and hygiene practices. Graduates are prepared for employment in salons, spas, wellness centers, or self-employment in the beauty industry.

  • Professional behavior and client care

Module 2: Skin Care
