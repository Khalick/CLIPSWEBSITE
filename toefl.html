Course Overview

The TOEFL Preparation course equips learners with the skills and strategies to succeed in the Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL). It focuses on the four tested areas: Reading, Listening, Speaking, and Writing. Students develop English language proficiency and exam confidence through structured practice, feedback, and simulation exams.
