Course Overview

The Diploma in ICT (Information and Communication Technology) course equips learners with practical and theoretical knowledge in computing, networking, programming, databases, cybersecurity, and systems analysis. It prepares students for professional roles in the IT industry or to pursue further studies in software engineering, systems administration, or data science.
