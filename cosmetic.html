Course Overview

The Cosmetelogy and Nail Technology course offers comprehensive training in nail artistry, cosmetic science, skincare, and salon management. Learners acquire skills in manicure and pedicure techniques, acrylic and gel nail systems, nail art, cosmetic application, beauty product usage, and client service. The course blends practical experience with professional standards, preparing students for careers in beauty salons, spas, or as independent beauty entrepreneurs.
