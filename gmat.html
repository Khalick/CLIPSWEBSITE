Course Overview

The GMAT Preparation course is designed for individuals pursuing admission into graduate business schools. It focuses on core GMAT sections: Quantitative Reasoning, Verbal Reasoning, Integrated Reasoning, and Analytical Writing. The course offers practice tests, performance analytics, and strategy-based training to boost your score and readiness for top MBA programs.
