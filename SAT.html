Course Overview

The SAT Preparation course is designed to help students excel in the SAT exam used for university admissions. It focuses on key areas tested: Evidence-Based Reading, Writing & Language, Math (with and without calculator), and optional Essay. The course includes practice tests, exam strategies, and time management techniques to boost scores and build test confidence.
