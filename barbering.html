Course Overview

The Barbering course provides professional training in haircutting, shaving, grooming, and beard styling for men. The course covers both classic and modern techniques, hair and scalp care, hygiene standards, client consultation, and the use of barbering tools. It prepares learners for employment in barbershops or to start their own grooming business.
